Format Guidelines
Example Format:
A+ Trade Setups - Wed May 15

1. SPY - Rejection Near $590.50
   Bias: Bearish below $590
   Trigger: Short if breaks below $588.75
   Target: $586.50, $584.25

2. AAPL - Breakout Above $188.50
   Bias: Bullish above $188
   Trigger: Long if holds above $188.50
   Target: $190.25, $192.00

The system will automatically extract ticker symbols, signals, and price levels from your message.

Signal Types
  • Breakout Price breaking above a resistance level
  • Breakdown Price breaking below a support level
  • Rejection Price failing to break through a level
  • Bounce Price rebounding from a support/resistance
